## Automated Review Generation
Reviews are produced at scale using scripts, AI, or templates, often lacking real user experience.

## Paid or Incentivized Reviews
Individuals are compensated or rewarded to leave positive reviews without genuine use of the product.

## Review Reuse or Duplication
Identical or slightly modified reviews are published across multiple platforms or products.
