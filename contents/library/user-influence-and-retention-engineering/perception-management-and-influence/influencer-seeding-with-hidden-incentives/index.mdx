---
title: Influencer seeding with hidden incentives
description: Providing influencers with monetary or material benefits in exchange for promotion, without requiring them to disclose the nature of the compensation. The content is presented as authentic, independent opinion.
---

## Observed Types

### Undisclosed Sponsorships
Influencers promote products or services without informing their audience of paid arrangements.

### Gifted Promotions Framed as Genuine Use
Products or experiences are provided for free, but the influencer portrays them as independently chosen or purchased.
