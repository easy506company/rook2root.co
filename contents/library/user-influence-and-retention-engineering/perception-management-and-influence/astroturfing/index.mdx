---
title: Astroturfing
description: Fabrication or orchestration of grassroots support or consensus. False signals of approval, satisfaction, or popularity are presented to manufacture social credibility and trust.
metaDescription: Creating fake grassroots support or consensus to manufacture credibility and trust through false signals of approval.
---

## Fake Endorsements
Testimonials, reviews, or ratings are generated without real user input, often using fictitious identities.

## Staged Community Support
Forums, comment sections, or social posts are seeded with coordinated positive feedback to simulate organic engagement.
