---
title: Friction-based churn suppression
description: Intentionally designed barriers, delays, or complications that make cancellation or disengagement difficult. The aim is to reduce user attrition by exhausting, confusing, or discouraging exit attempts.
---

## Hidden or Obscured Options
Cancellation or downgrade options are buried, mislabeled, or only accessible through support channels.

## Deliberate Delays
Processing times are extended or confirmation steps are introduced to prolong commitment.
