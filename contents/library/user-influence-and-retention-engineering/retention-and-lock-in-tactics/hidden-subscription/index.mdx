---
title: Hidden Subscription
description: Presenting recurring billing arrangements in a way that conceals their long-term financial commitment. The subscription model is introduced late, ambiguously, or under the guise of a free or one-time offer.
---

## Observed Types

### Disguised Recurrence
Subscription terms are buried in fine print or only revealed after checkout.

### Free Trial Trap
Users are enrolled into auto-renewing plans after a trial period, without clear advance warning or reminders.

### Ambiguous Pricing Labels
Interfaces display a one-time cost while recurring charges are only disclosed in secondary text or footnotes.
